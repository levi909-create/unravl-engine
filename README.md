# unravl-engine

Feed it art. Watch it decompose.

**[Try the live demo →](https://levi909-create.github.io/unravl-engine/)**

## What it does

UNRAVL Engine analyzes creative works and decomposes them into 10 core emotional dimensions. Then it translates those emotions into wildly specific descriptions across 6 different mediums:

- **Architecture** — raw concrete, impossible proportions, spaces that make you feel
- **Perfume** — top notes, heart, base; scent as emotional architecture
- **Choreography** — dancers, movement, stillness as meaning
- **Cuisine** — unnamed courses that decompose emotion into flavor
- **Fashion** — unwearable proportions, emotional forensics made wearable
- **Landscape** — physical spaces shaped by feeling

## Emotions

The engine analyzes 10 core emotional dimensions:

- Tension
- Yearning
- Wonder
- Melancholy
- Defiance
- Ecstasy
- Dread
- Tenderness
- Fury
- Serenity
